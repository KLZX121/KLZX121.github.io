About: I made this for myself to improve my Morse code skills, hence why it is really roughly put together. It basically gives you a random word (or letter depending on the mode you have chosen) and you use the hotkeys listed to type it out in Morse. Words mode gives you a random word, Random mode gives you a random letter, and Iterate mode just goes through the alphabet. A Morse chart can be found here.
