Instructions:

Tap or hold the Space key to input dots or dashes respectively when translating English to Morse, or type out the English letters when translating Morse to English.
The Phrase section displays the current phrase which you have to translate.
The Previous phrase section shows the previous given phrase and its correct translation.
The Your answer section shows your answer and its translation.
The Score section shows your total record.

About: I made this website for myself to practice Morse, so it is more focused on functionality than anything. It basically gives you a random word (or letter/number depending on the mode you have chosen) and you translate it in Morse, or vice versa. A Morse chart can be found here.

Settings

Languages:

  • English: English Output - Translate to Morse
  • Morse: Morse Output - Translate to English
Types:
  • Words: Outputs a word from a list of the 1000 most common English words (Credits)
  • Letters: A single letter from the English alphabet
  • Numbers: A number from 0 - 9 if iterating or a number from 0 - 999999 if random
Modes:
  • Random: Chooses from the selected list randomly
  • Iterate: Runs through the selected list one by one
Auto Space: Automatically add a space after each word in your Morse input
Auto Space Delay: The delay after your last input before a space is added
Auto Dash Delay: The length of time you need to hold the space key to input a dash

Hotkeys:

Space = short press ( dot · ) / long press ( dash − )

, (Comma) = space

Enter = submit

LControl = skip word

Backspace = delete last character
